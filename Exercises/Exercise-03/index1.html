Ethereal off-screen system

Ethereal is a phone notification filtering and rearranging system. Phone notifications can be distractive while users concentrating on productivity or interpersonal communication. The aim of this project was to tangibly study a playful experience that would offer users a choice that taking control of their phone.

The physical part of Ethereal is a smartphone pop sticker integrated with a built-in control knob and an LED signal light. It pares with the user's smartphone via Bluetooth to perform tasks such as filtering in-coming notifications, silencing the unwanted, irrelevant push notifications, and highlighting the important/emergency messages.
